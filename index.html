Once upon a time there was a Princess. And this Princess’s dad and mom (the King and Queen) somehow got it into their royal heads that no Prince would be good enough for their girl unless he could feel a pea through one hundred mattresses.

So it should come as no surprise that the Princess had a very hard time finding a Prince. Every time she met a nice boy, her mom and dad would pile one hundred mattresses on top of a pea and then invite him to sleep over.

When the Prince came down for breakfast, the Queen would ask, “How did you sleep, dear?” The Prince would politely say, “Fine, thank you.” And the King would show him the door.

Now this went on for three years. And of course nobody ever felt the pea under one hundred mattresses. Then one day, the Princess met the guy of her dreams. She decided she better do something about it. That night, before the Prince went to bed, the Princess slipped her [item one] under the one hundred mattresses.

When the Prince came down for breakfast the next moring, the Queen asked, “How did you sleep, dear?” The Prince seemed a little cramped but politely said, “Fine, thank you.” The King and Queen saw his discomfort and decided to grant him another chance.

After spending the entire day together, the Princess thought of what else she could use to make the Prince feel even more uncomfortable. That night, before the Prince went to bed, the Princess slipped her [item two] under the one hundred mattresses.

When the Prince limped down the stairs for breakfast the next moring, the Queen asked, “How did you sleep, dear?” The Prince was clearly distressed but politely grumbled, “Okay, thanks.” Again, the King and Queen saw his discomfort and decided to grant him one last chance.

The Princess was thrilled, but terrified. She .poured over. what else she could use to bring the Prince excruciating amounts of pain. That night, before the Prince went to bed, the Princess slipped her [item three] under the one hundred mattresses.

When the prince staggered down the stairs for breakfast the next morning, the Queen asked, “How did you sleep, dear?” “This might sound odd,” groaned the prince. “But I think you need another mattress. I felt like I was sleeping on a lump as big as a [item three].”

The King and Queen were satisfied. The Princess and Prince were married. And everyone lived happily, though maybe not completely honestly, ever after. The End.
